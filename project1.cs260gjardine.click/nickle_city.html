Nickle City Info

Cheap, Close and Fun

If you're looking for a super fun place to go, but don't have a lot of money this is the place for you. All the arcade games there cost 5 cents, so $10 will get you pretty far. If you're looking for food that is close and cheap as well Wendy's is just down the road. This place also has the Gideon guarentee. You won't regret going here for a first date.
