BYU Bowling Info

Activities:

One of my favorite things to do at BYU bowling is pool. Not only is it a slow paced game that gives you time to get to know your date, but it's free as well! Pool is also a great way to start a friendly/flirtatious competition.

Another fun activity to is the bowling itself. I would suggest that you start with bowling and then go to play pool. I do this because it's loud in the alley and hard to start up a conversation, but its a good ice breaker. Playing pool after will help you start a meaningful conversation and get to know your date better.

Lastly I would suggest that you go to the creamery for some good ol fashioned ice cream. The ice cream is cheap and it is delicious. You can decide to do this or not depending on how the date is going. If it is going great by all means go get some ice cream.
